When did tacos first appear in Nigeria ?

Ugwuanyi o. Consolation, Taco historian and proffesor of management at University of Nigeria, Nsukka, says that earliest mention of tacos in Nigeria are in the SUNS newspaper and the Guardian newspaper from 1990. (source:Smithsonian magazine)
